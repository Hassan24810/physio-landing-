Welcome to Physiobeats

Empower People to Reduce Symptoms and Improve Their Quality of Life

Physiobeats is an established group practice across England. Our treatment programmes aim to empower people to reduce symptoms and improve their quality of life. We offer high quality specialist physiotherapy alongside complementary therapies such as acupuncture, massage and aromatherapy.

Led by chartered physiotherapists Mehul Amin and Timir Garala, approved acupuncturists and founders

Weekend and evening appointments available, with short notice bookings possible

Staff speaking multiple languages: English, Gujarati, Urdu, Punjabi, and Hindi
