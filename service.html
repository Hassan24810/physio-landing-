What We Do

Our Comprehensive Physiotherapy Services

Our expert physiotherapists provide comprehensive care using evidence-based treatments and personalized rehabilitation programs. We specialize in a wide range of therapeutic approaches to help you achieve optimal physical function and wellbeing. With state-of-the-art facilities and a patient-centered approach, we're committed to delivering the highest quality physiotherapy services.

Back & Neck Pain

We use holistic approach with specialist physiotherapy techniques and electrotherapy in management of Back and Neck pain.

Whiplash Injury Rehabilitation

We specialise in fast-track rehab pathway for whiplash Injury and help you to return to you normal ability quicker.

Ultrasound & TENs Therapy

Improve your soft tissue healing with Ultrasound Therapy and reduce your pain with TENs Therapy.

Heat & Soft Tissue Therapy

Reduce muscle spasm and pain with Heat Therapy and soft tissue manipulations for improved mobility.

Trigger Point & Joint Therapy

Release trigger points and reduce joint stiffness with specialized mobilization techniques.

Acupuncture & Hand Therapy

Qualified physiotherapists providing acupuncture for pain management and specialist hand therapy rehabilitation.

Neurological Rehabilitation

Specialist neurological rehabilitation for conditions including Stroke, Head injury, Multiple Sclerosis, Parkinson's etc.

Vestibular & Occupational Rehab

Specialized vestibular rehabilitation and occupational therapy to help you return to work sooner.
